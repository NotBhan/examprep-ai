# **App Name**: ExamPrep AI

## Core Features:

- Syllabus Deconstructor: Upload exam syllabus (PDF, .txt) for AI parsing to create an interactive syllabus mind map. The AI tool estimates topic weightage for importance.
- Dynamic Study Planner: Generates a personalized study schedule based on exam date, study hours, style, and intensity. Includes topic allocation, revision scheduling, and buffer days.
- On-Demand Quiz Engine: Generates quizzes on selected topics with customizable difficulty and number of questions.  AI tool creates explanations for each answer.
- Context-Aware Concept Tutor: AI chatbot that answers syllabus-related questions, bounded by the uploaded syllabus content. The AI tool provides explanations, examples, and comparisons based on student's prompts.
- Personalized Calendar View: View study plan in a calendar format, with options to export to Google Calendar or iCal.
- Syllabus Tree View: Browse topics and subtopics from your syllabus in collapsible tree or mindmap format.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark teal (#008080), inspired by a sense of knowledge, focus and preparation.
- Background color: Very dark gray (#222222), for a high-contrast dark mode, reducing eye strain during long study sessions.
- Accent color: Pale turquoise (#AFEEEE), to provide contrast on key elements.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' sans-serif; body font: 'Inter' sans-serif. Use Space Grotesk for headers and Inter for body text.
- Use clean, minimalist icons for easy navigation and quick understanding of features.
- Maintain a clean and intuitive layout to ensure a seamless and focused user experience.
- Employ subtle transitions and loading animations to provide feedback without distracting from the core learning process.
